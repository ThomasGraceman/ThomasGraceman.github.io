This book develops the theory of stochastic processes from a geometric perspective, with a particular emphasis on bounding the suprema of random processes. In particular, the techniques developed in this book provide powerful tools for understanding concentration phenomena and the behavior of random structures that appear throughout asymptotic convex geometry and theoretical computer science.
