--- title: "$\ell_p$ Row Sampling by Lewis Weights" collection: paper paperurl: "https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~jrl/papers/pdf/norm-sparsify.pdf" --- This paper studies row sampling in $\ell_p$ spaces through the notion of Lewis weights. I am interested in it because Lewis weights are one of the main tools behind several modern sparsification results, including the recent paper of James R. Lee and his collaborators on sparsifying sums of norms.
